Gilbert's Decisions

Do you count punctuation or only words?

I only count words. This decision was made because it would yield the fastest results for us to truly differentiate language.


Which words should matter in similarity comparison?

This application removes common stop words, so that only the differences can be analyzed.


Do you care about word ordering?

My application does not care about the ordering of words.


What metric will you use to assign numerical value to similarity?

The metric used for text similarity is the equation: # of unique matches / total unique words in both samples


What are the data structures and algorithms you used to complete the task?

Dictionaries, lists, and sets were employed. The sets were used for comparing unique words between two samples and are helpful because they execute better than lists.
